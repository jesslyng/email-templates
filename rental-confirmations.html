Important Information
 
Security Deposity
 
Prior to your rental, we require a security hold of $50 if you have a US drivers license ($250 for international drivers license and drivers under 25) be placed on a credit or charge card in your name. Debit cards are also accepted if you provide proof of a same-day inbound flight. You do not need to use the same card you used for initial payment. -- The hold expires within 7 days of the end of your rental.
 
Rental Dropoff
 
To ensure that your car is available for the owner, please return it to the pickup location at the scheduled drop-off time (late returns will result in additional fees). We will arrange for your transportation back to the airport.
 
Insurance
 
Please review the insurance coverage and policies details to understand your liabilities. This document also provides you information on what is included in the basic coverage and what you have to pay extra for. If your age is under 25 you will have to purchase our premium insurance at $18.00 per day which can be waived if you have proof of a valid insurance. Renters with non US drivers license will have to purchase our premium insurance at $18.00 per day.
